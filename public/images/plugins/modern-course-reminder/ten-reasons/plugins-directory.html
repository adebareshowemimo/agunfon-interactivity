Modern Course Reminder

Modern Course Reminder turns learner follow-up into an automated engine. Instead of teachers chasing learners by hand, rules detect who has not started, not finished, gone inactive or missed a deadline — and send the right reminder on the right channel, on cron.

On that foundation it escalates still-incomplete learners to their line manager, drafts branded templates (optionally with AI), batches manager digests, and reports on what actually moved completion — with filterable, exportable logs and a Report Builder datasource for audit.

Supports Moodle 4.5–5.2.

Moodle 4.5–5.2 GPLv3 Stable

All features

Rules & triggers

  • Triggers for not-started, course-incomplete, inactivity and deadline approaching/overdue
  • Scope a rule to a single course, a whole category, a cohort, or site-wide
  • Target overall course completion or specific completion-tracked activities (multi-select)
  • Preview the exact learners a rule matches before it sends
  • Ready-made starter rules: not started, incomplete, inactive, deadline, overdue-with-escalation

Course-level management & guided setup

  • Teachers manage reminder rules from inside their own course
  • A setup checklist guides completion tracking, criteria and activity completion
  • Rules that target completion are blocked until the course is correctly configured

Sending & campaigns

  • One-off manual send to a course with audience filters and a recipient preview
  • Eligible and skipped recipients shown with reasons before anything sends
  • One-off and recurring scheduled campaigns (daily / weekly / monthly)
  • Repeat cadence with a maximum-reminders cap
  • Campaign start/stop dates and a daily send window control exactly when sends go out

Manager escalation

  • Resolve a learner's line manager three ways: Moodle manager role, custom profile field, or a manual mapping table
  • Bulk-import learner→manager mappings from CSV
  • Escalate on days overdue, number of reminders received, or a fixed compliance date
  • Dedicated manager-escalation templates and report

Templates & content

  • Reusable templates for learner, manager and digest audiences
  • ~25 placeholders (name, course, due date, days overdue, manager, dashboard URL and more)
  • Brand colours and an email-safe logo that resolves to an absolute URL
  • Sandboxed preview with sample data before anything sends
  • Optional AI drafting via Moodle's own AI subsystem — no API keys stored in the plugin

Channels & delivery

  • Email and Moodle notification, selectable per rule and gated by site-wide switches
  • Batched manager/admin digests — daily, weekly or monthly
  • Per-run throttle, configurable retries and retry delay

Automation & operations

  • Seven scheduled tasks: evaluate → build queue → send → retry → digests → cleanup → refresh
  • De-duplication so a learner is never spammed
  • Queue viewer to cancel or requeue individual items

Analytics & reporting

  • Dashboard cards: active rules, sent today/this week, pending queue, failed, overdue learners, escalations
  • Effectiveness analytics: learners reminded, completed-after-reminder, average days to complete, repeats
  • Delivery, overdue, inactive, rule-performance and manager-escalation reports
  • Filterable, exportable audit logs
  • Report Builder datasource for the reminder delivery log

Safety & trust

  • Learner opt-out and automatic skipping of suspended / no-email / exempt users
  • Built-in health check for cron, email, queue, tasks, manager mapping, templates and AI
  • Capability-based access for admins and teachers

Platform & privacy

  • Built on core Moodle APIs (Completion, Enrolment, Messaging) — no external service to host
  • Full GDPR privacy provider with user export and deletion
  • Moodle 4.5–5.2, GPLv3
  • Drop-in successor to local_course_reminder with a CLI data migration
