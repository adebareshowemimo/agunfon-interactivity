Modern Course Reminder

Modern Course Reminder turns learner follow-up into an automated engine. Instead of teachers chasing learners by hand, rules detect who has not started, not finished, gone inactive or missed a deadline — and send the right reminder on the right channel, on cron.

On that foundation it escalates still-incomplete learners to their line manager, drafts branded templates (optionally with AI), batches manager digests, and reports on what actually moved completion — with filterable, exportable logs and a Report Builder datasource for audit.

Supports Moodle 4.5–5.2.

Moodle 4.5–5.2 GPLv3 Stable
