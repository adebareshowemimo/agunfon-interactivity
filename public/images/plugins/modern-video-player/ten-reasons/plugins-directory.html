Modern Video Player

Modern Video Player turns an ordinary video into a controlled, Moodle-native activity with playback that is validated on the server — so completion and watch-time actually mean something.

On that foundation it adds engagement heatmaps and a cohort analytics dashboard, signed-URL delivery with dynamic watermarking, cloud and provider sources, seven branded player skins, learner aids like resume, chapters, transcript and captions, and audit-ready exports — all GPLv3 and upgradeable from the free Community edition in place.

Supports Moodle 4.5–5.2.

12 reasons to choose Modern Video Player

All features

Playback & integrity

  • Server-validated watch-time and max-verified position
  • Forward-seek guard with configurable grace seconds
  • Heartbeat tracking and suspicious-seek integrity flags
  • Force server-side validation

Completion & grading

  • Complete by watched percentage or validated end-of-video
  • Configurable required watch percentage (default 95%)
  • Gradebook integration with a grade-to-pass threshold

Learner experience

  • Resume exactly where the learner left off
  • Clickable WebVTT chapter markers and chapter list
  • Searchable transcript panel with optional download
  • Multi-language WebVTT captions
  • Learner bookmarks with labels
  • Adjustable playback speed (with a max-speed cap), rewind and fullscreen
  • Autoplay (off / muted / unmuted with fallback) and poster image
  • Next-activity overlay — replay or continue to the next activity
  • Keyboard shortcuts with a shortcuts-help dialog

Engagement analytics

  • Per-activity engagement heatmap and audience-retention curve
  • Viewers-per-moment with drop-off and rewatch peaks
  • Site-level cohort dashboard aggregating every activity, with a cohort filter
  • Scheduled engagement digests emailed to instructors

Compliance & audit

  • Full audit export as CSV, JSON, Excel or ODS
  • Per-learner integrity records and validated watched segments

Content protection

  • Dynamic name/email watermark overlay
  • Screen-capture deterrence with a tamper-evident audit log
  • Signed, user-bound video URLs that expire

Sources & delivery

  • File upload, external direct URL (MP4/WebM), YouTube and Vimeo
  • Cloud storage: S3-compatible (AWS S3, Cloudflare R2, Wasabi, Spaces, B2, MinIO) and Azure Blob
  • Short-lived signed delivery from your own bucket
  • Admin source-policy guardrails

Appearance

  • Seven player styles: YouTube, Vimeo, Netflix, Plyr, Apple, Classic and Minimal
  • Per-activity style choice with an admin-set default
  • Configurable title position and control-text toggle

Focus mode

  • Hide navigation, course index and right blocks during playback
  • Block Picture-in-Picture and skip shortcuts
  • Auto-pause when the browser tab is hidden

Standards & integration

  • xAPI statements (initialized, completed, flagged) to any LRS
  • LTI 1.3 Advantage publishing via core enrol_lti
  • Activity events and logs for clean reporting

Platform & trust

  • Moodle 4.5–5.2, GPLv3
  • Privacy API (GDPR) — declares every learner data point stored
  • Full backup and restore of activities, files and progress
  • Open-core: upgrade the free Community edition in place
