# Agunfon Design System

## Brand Positioning
- Intelligent, Modern, Trustworthy
- Enterprise EdTech SaaS
- Structured, calm, premium tone
- Avoid playful or flashy visuals

## Color System
**Primary:** Deep Blue `#022B69`
**Secondary:** Alice Blue `#EEF6FF`
**Text:** Eerie Black `#181818`
**Background:** White `#FFFFFF`

**Color Ratios:**
- 60% White
- 25% Deep Blue
- 10% Alice Blue
- 5% Eerie Black accents

## Typography
**Primary (Headings):** Sora
**Secondary (Body):** Inter

**Hierarchy:**
- H1: 48–64px, Bold
- H2: 36–42px, Bold
- H3: 28–32px, SemiBold
- H4: 22–24px, Medium
- P1: 16–18px, Regular
- P2: 14–16px, Regular
- Buttons: 14–16px, Medium

**Line Height:**
- Headings: 110–120%
- Body: 140–160%

**Letter Spacing:**
- Headings: -0.5%
- Body: +0.2%

## Layout & Grid
- Desktop: 12-column grid, 24px gutter, 5–8% margin
- Tablet: 8-column grid, 20px gutter
- Mobile: 4-column grid, 16px gutter

## Components
**Buttons:**
- Primary: Deep Blue background, white text, radius 6–8px
- Secondary: Alice Blue background, Deep Blue text, 1px border
- Tertiary: Text-only, Deep Blue

**Cards:**
- Background: White or Alice Blue
- Radius: 12px
- Shadow: subtle

**Forms:**
- Border: 1px Eerie Black @ 20%
- Focus: Deep Blue border
- Labels: Inter 14px

## Logo Usage
- Clear space: 20–40% of logo height
- No gradients or shadow
- Minimum size: 24px logo
- Placement: Top-left (dashboard), top-center (marketing)

## Pattern Usage
- Giraffe abstract pattern only in subtle overlays
- Opacity 5–10% (background), max 15% (hero)

## Visual Tone
- No heavy gradients
- No bright saturated colors
- No excessive shadows
- No cartoon icons
- Clean, structured, enterprise look
