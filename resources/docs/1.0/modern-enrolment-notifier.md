# Modern Enrolment Notifier

---

**Modern Enrolment Notifier** (`local_modernenrolnotifier`) sends clear, timely notifications when learners are enrolled, before their access expires, and when they complete a course. Rules decide *when* a message sends, *who* receives it, *which* template renders it, and *which* channel carries it — and everything is queued and delivered by Moodle cron, so enrolment activity stays fast.
